About Us

Learn more about our mission, vision, and story.

Ujjwal Path Foundation is a non-profit organisation dedicated to empowering underprivileged children through education and community support. We believe that meaningful change begins when communities come together with empathy, knowledge, and the courage to act.

Our mission is to create safe, inclusive spaces where children and their families can access the support, resources, and opportunities they deserve. Through after-school programmes, mentorship, and awareness campaigns, we work to bridge gaps in learning and well-being.

From small neighbourhood learning circles to city-wide initiatives, our story is shaped by volunteers, partners, and supporters who share one simple belief: every child deserves a fair chance to build a brighter future.

Our mission in one line

To empower underprivileged children and their communities through holistic education, mentorship, and compassionate support.
